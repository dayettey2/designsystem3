Typography

Merri-weather Display & Open Sans

The typographical hierarchy implemented within this design system combines serif and sans-serif fonts, Merriweather and Open-Sans. The heading incorporates Serif font and the body is sans-serif font. This typographical hierarchy is arranged using a Major Third scale with a base font size of 16px.
