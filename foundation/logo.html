Logo Variations

The colored Logo features two women of color in a reflective view holding hands. I chose to design the Logo that way to evoke a sense of unity, support, community, and resourcefulness as women are going through prenatal and postpartum pregnancy during and after birth. This Logo also features a single-lined circle that can be used on print and digital materials. The colored version of the Logo uses a color scheme that pairs well against a lighter or darker background.
